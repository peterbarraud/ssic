Our School

Sri Satyanarayan Inter College (SSIC) Shamli is a unique institution. It was established in 1978. The foundation stone was laid by late Ilam Chand Goyal ji and late Trilok Chand Gangal ji. Since then it has developed into a full-fledged institution. It satisfies the aim of providing education, socialism, national sprit to make responsible citizen for the future. The school imparts the education to develop the body, mind, character and the quality of leadership.

The institution is run by Shri Satyanaryan Siksha Samiti, Shamli.

The school imparts education in the most modern lines with Indian cultural background. The school is situated in a quiet and peaceful location with natural green surroundings.

SSIC is one of the premier educational institutions in the city. As the torchbearers of knowledge and growth, we leave no stone unturned in providing an extraordinary learning experience to our students.

The best of infrastructural facilities, high-tech labs, well-stocked library and vast playgrounds, etc. – the prerequisites that make for a wonderful school life – are available, by default, to all SSICians.

Our team of highly experienced and dynamic teachers believes in imparting knowledge the unconventional way.

Our academic pursuits, along with a range of extracurricular activities, help in honing a child’s skills and ensuring that he/she grows to be a mature and responsible citizen.
