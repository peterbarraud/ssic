Social Sc. Lab

The Social Science “Heritage” Lab is an extremely hip and happening place. A beautiful and sunlit room, it has shelves filled with clay models of various landforms, miniature, bronze historical figurines and lots of wall mounted models and maps-all of which are used extensively by both students and teachers. Projects and role plays by students are presented here, eco-symposiums are held, geography practical of the XI and XII classes are all held in the lab.
